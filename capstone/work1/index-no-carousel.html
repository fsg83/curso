About

This website was created as my final project for the Web Design course from the University of Michigan on Coursera. Throughout the course, I delved into the fundamentals of web design, including concepts of usability, accessibility, and responsive layouts. This project represents my growth as a designer and developer, applying in practice everything I’ve learned. My goal with this website is not only to showcase the skills I’ve acquired but also to create something functional, visually appealing, and accessible to all users.
